Health

Your character has Health Points equal to their Constitution + half their Strength

Healing

Each night you rest Heal 1 point

  • +1 if you have a proper dinner
  • +1 if resting in town
  • +3 if being taken care of by a doctor

Deaths Door

A character with 0 HP is on Deaths Door. They may fight on without penalty however any time they would take damage Roll 1d6 on a 3 or lower they die

Checks

When a character attempts something they may fail at roll 1d20. Rolling under a relevent tatistic indicates a succsess whil rolling over is a failure

Degrees of succses

When rolling a check count the diferance between your roll and your statistic. This number is your degree of succses or of failure

Dificulty

  • Easy - +5 to your stat
  • normal
  • Hard - roll under half your statistic
  • Very Hard - roll under a quater of your statistic

Equipment

Weapons

All weapons deal 1d6 damage

For Melee weapons

  • if the weapon needs two hands roll twice and take the higher damage
  • if the weapon is sharp +1 to hit
  • if the weapon is really heavy -2 to hit +2 to damage
  • if the weapon is made by a master +2 to hit
  • if the weapon is some junk you found laying around it deals half damage

For Ranged weapons

  • if the weapon uses gunpowder it deals 2d6 damage and takes a turn to reload. Rolling snake eyes means it blows up in your hands
  • if the weapon is made by a master +2 to hit

Armour

Armour gives DT and DR if you have both DT and DR apply DT before DR
